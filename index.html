What is Gold Coin?

DIGITAL
GOLD
REIMAGINED

Gold has been humanity's ultimate store of value for over 5,000 years. Scarce by nature. Impossible to inflate. Trusted across every civilization and culture.

Gold Coin (GDC) takes this ancient philosophy into the digital age. Built on BNB Smart Chain, GDC is a deflationary token that mirrors gold's most powerful property — an ever-decreasing, ever-scarcer supply.

Every transaction burns 2% of GDC permanently. Every trade makes the remaining supply more valuable. Every holder benefits as time passes — exactly like physical gold, but digital, borderless, and accessible to anyone on Earth.

With a hard cap of 21,000,000 GDC — inspired by Bitcoin's legendary scarcity — Gold Coin is built for those who think in decades, not days.

Got Questions?

FREQUENTLY ASKED

What is Gold Coin (GDC)?+
Gold Coin (GDC) is a deflationary BEP-20 token on BNB Smart Chain. It is inspired by gold's philosophy of scarcity and long-term value storage. With a fixed supply of 21 million GDC and a 2% automatic burn on every transaction, GDC is designed to become scarcer — and potentially more valuable — over time.
Is GDC safe from rug pulls?+
Yes. At launch, all LP tokens are permanently burned — meaning no one can ever remove liquidity from the trading pool. Additionally, ownership of the smart contract is renounced after launch, so no one can modify the contract, pause trading, or change any parameters. The code governs GDC, not any individual or team.
How does the 2% burn work?+
Every time GDC is transferred — whether it's a buy, sell, or wallet-to-wallet transfer — 2% of the transaction amount is automatically sent to the zero address (0x000...000) and permanently destroyed. This is executed by the smart contract code automatically, with no human involvement. Over time, this reduces the total circulating supply continuously.
What are the buy and sell taxes?+
Buy tax is 4% total: 2% goes to burn (permanently destroyed) and 2% goes to the liquidity pool to strengthen price stability. Sell tax is 6% total: 2% goes to burn, 2% to liquidity, and 2% to the marketing wallet to fund growth, listings, and community development. These taxes are the maximum and will never increase.
Why is the supply capped at 21 million?+
21 million is inspired by Bitcoin's legendary hard cap — the most recognized number in crypto for representing absolute scarcity. Just as Bitcoin's 21 million cap makes it "digital gold," GDC's 21 million cap reinforces its identity as a deflationary, scarce digital asset. With the 2% burn, the actual circulating supply will be far less than 21 million over time.
Where can I buy GDC?+
GDC will be available on PancakeSwap V3 on BNB Smart Chain from day one. You will need BNB in a MetaMask wallet, then swap BNB for GDC using the contract address. Set your slippage to 6-8% to account for the transaction taxes. Future listings on CoinGecko, CoinMarketCap, MEXC, and Gate.io are on the roadmap.
Is there a whitepaper?+
Yes. The full GDC whitepaper covers the project vision, problem statement, technical architecture, tokenomics, security model, and 4-phase roadmap in detail. It is available for download from this website. The smart contract source code is fully open-source and verified on BscScan for anyone to review.
